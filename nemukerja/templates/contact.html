Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Register" button in the top navigation and choose between Job Seeker or Company account.

Is NemuKerja free to use?

Yes! Both job seekers and companies can use our basic services completely free of charge.

How do I apply for jobs?

Browse available jobs, click "View Details" and then "Apply Now" to submit your application with a cover letter.

Can I post multiple job openings?

Yes, companies can post unlimited job openings and manage them through the company dashboard.

How long does it take to get a response?

Companies typically respond within 3-5 business days, but this may vary depending on the employer.

Do you have a mobile app?

Our website is fully responsive and works perfectly on mobile devices. A dedicated mobile app is coming soon!
